# Privacy Policy for Athlink

**Last Updated: November 20, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Athlink ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using Athlink, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information
We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and password
- **Profile Information**: Athletic profile details, sports categories, position, achievements, ratings, profile photos, and country information
- **Company Information**: For sponsors - company name, logo, and business details
- **Identity Verification**: Government-issued ID or other verification documents (if required)

### 1.2 Athletic and Professional Data
- Sports performance data and statistics
- Achievement records and certifications
- Job posts and sponsorship applications
- Sponsorship agreements and contract details
- Payment and budget information

### 1.3 Content You Create
- Job postings and descriptions
- Messages and communications with other users
- Photos, videos, and other media files you upload
- Comments, reviews, and ratings

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent on app, interaction patterns
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address (precise location only with your permission)
- **Log Data**: Error logs, crash reports, and diagnostic information

### 1.5 Audio Data
- Voice messages and audio recordings (only when you use audio messaging features)
- Audio is recorded only with your explicit permission

### 2.1 Core Services

### 2.2 Service Improvement

- Develop new features and services

- Conduct research and analytics

### 2.3 Communication
- Send you service-related notifications
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Send updates about your applications and sponsorships
- Provide important policy or terms updates

### 2.4 Safety and Security
- Verify user identities and prevent fraud
- Detect and prevent abuse, spam, or illegal activities
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Protect the rights and safety of our users

### 2.5 Legal Compliance
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to legal requests and prevent harm
- Protect our legal rights
